# Prophet Mesh Pricing and Packaging

## Packaging principle

Sell Michael Agent as the flagship product. Sell premium customer agents as Michael-derived deployments with customer-specific memory, connectors, workflows, policies, and operating boundaries.

## Editions

| Edition | Buyer | Included | Commercial motion |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Michael Agent Preview | Individual operators and early teams | Canonical Michael Agent, local/project memory, evidence-first reasoning, lifecycle trace visibility | Invite-only preview |
| Michael Agent Pro | Professionals and small teams | Michael Agent, persistent memory, governed planning, evidence packets, project workspaces | Self-serve or assisted onboarding |
| Prophet Mesh Premium | Teams with domain workflows | Custom Michael-derived agent, customer blueprint, domain vocabulary, memory connectors, workflow bindings, policy pack | Paid implementation plus recurring subscription |
| Prophet Mesh Enterprise | Regulated and infrastructure-heavy organizations | Private mesh deployment, identity integration, tenant isolation, audit exports, custom policy, support | Enterprise SOW plus annual contract |

## Metering axes

- Seats and named operators.
- Number of customer-derived agents.
- Memory graph size and connector count.
- Tool/workflow invocation volume.
- Evidence and attestation retention window.
- Deployment topology: managed, private, hybrid, or local-first.
- Support and governance review cadence.

## Premium implementation phases

1. Discovery and authority mapping.
2. Michael-derived agent design.
3. Memory and connector integration.
4. Tool/workflow binding.
5. Policy and approval design.
6. Evaluation harness setup.
7. Deployment and attestation review.
8. Operator enablement and managed runtime.

## Non-discountable boundaries

Do not discount away identity, policy, evidence, attestation, revocation, audit, tenant isolation, or lifecycle semantics. These are trust-kernel invariants and define the product category.

## Sales qualification questions

- What decisions or workflows require evidence trails?
- Which actions require human approval?
- Which systems must the agent read from or act through?
- What customer data may be retained, summarized, or exported?
- What audit, compliance, or retention obligations apply?
- Is deployment managed, private cloud, hybrid, or local-first?

## Launch path

1. Ship Michael Agent preview to trusted operators.
2. Convert high-fit preview users into premium design partners.
3. Package repeatable premium integrations into domain kits.
4. Use enterprise demand to harden identity, policy, audit, and deployment controls.
